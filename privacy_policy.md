# BoxBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to BoxBuddy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our BoxBuddy mobile application ("the App"). This privacy policy outlines our practices for handling your information and your rights regarding that information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Box Information**: Details about your boxes and their contents
- **Room Information**: Custom room names and organization data
- **Photos**: Images of boxes and items (stored locally on your device)
- **QR Codes**: Generated and scanned QR code data

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: 
  - Device model and Android version
  - Screen resolution and display settings
  - App performance metrics
- **Location Data**: 
  - Approximate location (city-level) for weather information
  - Not used for tracking or marketing
- **Usage Data**: 
  - App interaction statistics via Firebase Analytics
  - Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics
  - Feature usage patterns (anonymized)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Core Functionality
- Manage and organize your moving boxes
- Generate and scan QR codes
- Store and display box photos
- Provide weather information for moving conditions

### 3.2 App Improvement
- Analyze app performance
- Identify and fix crashes
- Improve user experience
- Optimize app features

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- All box information is stored locally on your device
- Photos are saved only on your device
- No cloud storage of personal data
- Optional backup feature for data preservation

### 4.2 External Services
- **Firebase Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: Crash reporting
- **OpenWeatherMap API**: Weather data based on approximate location
- No data sharing with third parties

## 5. Permissions

### 5.1 Required Permissions
- **Camera**: For QR code scanning and photo capture
- **Storage**: For saving box photos locally
- **Location**: For local weather information (approximate only)
- **Internet**: For crash reporting and weather data

### 5.2 Optional Permissions
- All permissions can be managed through your device settings
- The app will still function with limited features if permissions are denied

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- Access all your data within the app
- Export your data in a readable format
- Delete all app data from your device
- Manage app permissions
- Opt-out of analytics collection

## 7. Children's Privacy

- The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13
- No age-restricted content or features
- No social networking features

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last updated" date
- Notifying you via app update notes
